Talk Synopsis:

Kendra Initiative Case Study:

- Developing an open cross-vendor content distribution system facilitating revenue generation for content owners/aggregators and service providers
- Enabling the consumer to get what they want, when they want and pay for it
- The importance of first examining business models and interfaces in order to then define technological requirements
- Metadata content tagging, rights management and payment system transaction interoperability
- Multicasting, edge-caching and peer-to-peer networking coherently working together
- The impact of file sharing systems on revenue gained from content
- The whole content value chain from owner to consumer
- The benefits of such an open system to the consumer and all content industries
